Cheese Pizza

Description

Big 'ol yummy, cheesey pie.

Ingrediants

  • 1 Ball of Pizza Dough
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Semolina Flour
  • 3oz Mozzerella Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Basic Pizza Sauce
    • 15oz Can of Tomato Sauce
    • 2 tbsp. Extra Virgin Olive Oil
    • 2 Medium Garlic Cloves
    • 1-1/2 tsp. Chopped Fresh Oregano
    • 3/4 tsp. Fine Sea Salt
    • 1/4 tsp. Black Pepper
  • Fresh Basil or Oregano

The Process.

  1. Let chilled, covered dough warm at room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 500 degrees and allow pan to preheat for 30 minutes.
  3. Make Basic Sauce:
    1. Mix above ingredients in a bowl.
    2. Make a C-Shape with your hand and define a 1/2 inch wide ring around the edge of the dough. Lay dough round on a semolina-dusted pizza peel. Spread dough with Basic Pasta Sauce and cover with mozzerella.
    3. Gently shake pizza peel to loosen pie. Put pie into oven.
    4. Bake at 500 degrees for about 3 minutes.
    5. Enjoy!
