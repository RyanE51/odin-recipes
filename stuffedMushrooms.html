Stuffed Mushrooms

Description

Decadent, bite sized mushroom goodness stuffed with mouthwatering chesses.

Ingrediants

  • 12 whole, fresh, bite sized mushroom
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 8oz Cream Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Seasonings:
    • 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
    • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper
    • 1/4 tsp Onion Powder
    • 1/4 tsp Cayenne

The Process.

  1. Clean mushrooms and remove/chop stems.
  2. Cook the garlic and mushroom stems in oil and let cool.
  3. Stir in remaining ingrediants to make the filling.
  4. Fill each mushroom cap with the filling.
  5. Bake the stuffed mushrooms at 450 degrees for roughlhy 20 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
