My Morning Oatmeal

Description

Sweet and savory come together for this wholesome, filling breakfast you'll never grow tired of. Trust me, it's very tried and true.

Supplies

  • Peanut Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • One Banana
  • 1/2 cup of Oatmeal
  • Soy Milk

The Process.

  1. Scoop a half cup of oats into a bowl.
  2. Pour milk until it's just barely is at the level of your oats.
  3. Microwave for two minutes on high.
  4. Lather in as much cinnamon as your heart desires.
  5. Break up a banana and put it in the bowl.
  6. Scoop as much peanut butter as you want an put in the bowl with all the other goodies.
  7. Mix everything thoroughly.
  8. Enjoy!
